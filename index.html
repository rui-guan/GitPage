Welcome to my website!

I am a Lecturer in Economics at the University of Kent.

I hold a PhD in Economics from the Universitat Pompeu Fabra. My research in behavioral and experimental economics studies how cognitive limitations and institutional structures shape decision-making, with a focus on economic rationality, cooperation, and political participation.
